PAMP Suisse is recognized around the globe for its innovative designs, state-of-the-art facilities, and industry-leading efforts to curtail precious metal counterfeiting.

Bar Highlights:

  • Contains 1 oz. of .9999 pure gold.
  • Includes tamper-evident assay card to guarantee the Gold weight and purity.
  • PAMP’s exclusive Veriscan technology uses microscopic topography to identify any registered product. Each bar is scanned upon production and, when scanned is further authenticated by the Veriscan database.
  • Eligible for Precious Metals IRAs.

Veriscan is a unique technology developed by PAMP Suisse to help eliminate counterfeiting. The idea behind the system is simple. The microscopic topography of the surface of a gold bar makes it possible to assign an individual, unique fingerprint to each bar. When the bars are struck at the refinery, those fingerprints are recorded and catalogued.

The Fortuna gold-bar design is the most popular on a gold bullion bar today. The Roman goddess of fortune and personification of luck, Fortuna is often depicted with her eyes blindfolded or her face hidden behind a veil. It is not uncommon for a cornucopia to be included in depictions of Fortuna because, as Annonaria, she was considered the protector of grain supplies.

On the obverse side of these PAMP Suisse Fortuna 1 oz. Gold Bar is the image of Fortuna. Wearing a blindfold over her eyes and a cornucopia on her head, Fortunas clasped hands capture the overflowing bounty of riches from her cornucopia. The reverse side of the bar features the PAMP Suisse corporate logo, the bars weight, metal content, and purity, as well as the assayers mark and serial number.

PAMP Suisse, founded in Ticino, Switzerland in 1977, produces the Fortuna gold bar in weights ranging from .3 grams to 1 kilogram. This 1 oz. Fortuna Gold Bar is by far the most popular individual gold bar in the series. Today, PAMP Suisse is part of the larger MKS Group, based in Geneva. The company boasts over 50 years of experience in the precious metal and financial services industries.
